# PPC Guru — Competitor Gap Analysis & 1–3 Month Quick-Win SEO Plan

> **Status:** Planning only. **Nothing in this document has been built or changed on the website.**
> Prepared on branch `Siddharth`. Do not merge/implement to `main` without sign-off.
> **Author lens:** written as a 15+ yr Canadian digital-agency SEO strategist.
> **Horizon:** 1–3 months (fast wins), not a 6-month program.

---

## 0. How to read this

Every gap below is tagged with:
- **Who has it** — the real competitor(s) whose live pages/profiles show the element (attribution, as you asked).
- **Impact** — Ranking (R), Conversion (C), or both.
- **Effort** — S (days), M (1–2 wks), L (3–6 wks).
- **Honesty flag 🔒** — where PPC Guru's own honesty rules (no fabricated reviews/named clients; AggregateRating held until verified — see `CLAUDE.md`) mean the task is **"collect & display *real* proof,"** not "invent."

**Reality check from an SEO who's done this in Toronto:** PPC Guru's *on-page/technical/AEO* foundation is already strong (programmatic service/industry/location pages, FAQ + schema everywhere, /benchmarks Dataset, /glossary, /compare, free tools, llms.txt, AI-crawler access). That's ahead of most of the field. So in a 1–3 month window the needle-movers are **NOT more on-page content** — they are **(1) third-party trust signals, (2) bottom-funnel "money" pages, (3) off-site authority/citations, and (4) conversion instrumentation.** That's where competitors are beating us, and those are the fastest to close.

## 3. The gaps (attributed)

### A. Trust & social proof — **biggest lever, mostly missing**

1. **Verified third-party review widget + star rating.** 🔒 R+C · Effort M
   - **Who has it:** Awkward Media (**4.9★ / 87+** Google + TrustIndex badge on-page), dNovo (**5.0 / 42** Clutch), Consultus Digital (review widgets on service pages).
   - **We have:** aggregate stat numbers, but **no live Google/Clutch review stars**, and `AggregateRating` schema is deliberately OFF (honesty hold).
   - **Fix:** gather real Google Business Profile + Clutch reviews → embed a live rating widget → then (and only then) enable `Review`/`AggregateRating` schema for SERP star snippets. *Requires real reviews — not fabricated.*

2. **Named case studies with real client name + logo + documented metric.** 🔒 R+C · Effort M
   - **Who has it:** Awkward Media (KBL Law +94% traffic / +66% conversions; Grand Oaks Orthodontics +200% page-1 keywords; Attention2Detail top-3 in 60 days), dNovo (named legal/medical verticals).
   - **We have:** 6 **anonymized** case studies (by design). Anonymized converts worse and earns less trust than named+logo.
   - **Fix:** get written permission from 2–3 happy clients to name them + show logo + one hard metric. Highest-trust asset we can add.

3. **Third-party award / certification badges.** R+C · Effort S
   - **Who has it:** dNovo (Google Partner, **Semrush Certified Agency**, **Netty Award**, Clutch badges), TML (Google Partner), Make It Bloom (Meta Business Partner).
   - **We have:** Google/Meta Partner as booleans only, no verified badge links, no directory awards.
   - **Fix:** claim/complete Clutch, UpCity, DesignRush, Semrush Agency, hellodarwin profiles; display verifiable badges with links.

4. **Real client logo wall.** C · Effort S 🔒 — we use text-pill placeholders; competitors show real logos. (Needs real, permissioned logos.)

5. **Video (founder intro / client testimonial / 60-sec explainer).** R+C · Effort M
   - **Who has it:** most established players (Consultus, Bloom, Local SEO Search). We have none. Add `VideoObject` schema once live.

6. **Real team photos + expert author bios (E-E-A-T).** R · Effort S 🔒
   - **Who has it:** SEO Toronto (Paul Teitelman personal-brand authority), dNovo. We show monograms/placeholders. Google's E-E-A-T rewards real, credentialed authors on service + blog content.

### B. Offers & risk-reversal

7. **Named performance guarantee.** C · Effort S (copy/positioning)
   - **Who has it:** Awkward Media (60-day first-page + 20-day movement, **cash-backed**), GrowME (**ROI-in-12-months-or-free**), Propeller (money-back), SEP (results guarantee).
   - **We have:** a 30-day free trial (good, but scoped to Google/Meta ads). No SEO/results guarantee framing.
   - **Fix:** add an honest, defensible guarantee (e.g. "work free until X," or a reporting/relationship SLA). Must be real — no empty "we guarantee #1."

8. **Quantified free-audit value.** C · Effort S
   - **Who has it:** QliqQliq ("**$1,850 audit, free**"), MediaForce (free audit **+ $500 credit**), Wisdek/Consultus/BreezeMax.
   - **We have:** free audit exists but isn't value-anchored. Add a dollar-anchored "$X audit — free" frame.

### C. Pricing transparency

9. **Published pricing / "starting at" / package tiers.** R+C · Effort M
   - **Who has it:** Digital Shift ("**from $499/mo, no contract**"), SKYREX ("**$299/mo**"), AppWT ("**from $747**"), Qode Social (**named tiers w/ deliverables**), The Website Architect (**web-design package prices**).
   - **We have:** `/pricing` explains fee models but shows **no numbers** — so we lose the huge "[service] cost / pricing / how much" search cluster and the buyers who filter by price.
   - **Fix (honesty-safe):** publish transparent *ranges* or a "starting at" + wire the existing calculators into a "what you'll invest" band. Ranges are defensible without over-promising.

### D. Content — bottom-funnel "money" pages (**fastest ranking win**)

10. **"[Service] cost / pricing in Canada 2026" guides.** R · Effort M — **DO THIS FIRST**
    - **Who ranks with it:** Catmo ("Google Ads management cost"), GM Digital ("Digital marketing cost Canada 2026"), Town Media Labs, Chameleon Ideas ("Website design cost Toronto 2026"), Canada Create, RTOWN, Boomy.
    - **We have:** blog, but not the specific high-intent cost/pricing guides. These rank fast (lower competition than "[service] agency") and pull ready-to-buy traffic.

11. **"Best [service] agency in Toronto" comparison content + barnacle SEO.** R · Effort M
    - **Who ranks with it:** NoGood, Loopex, dNovo blog, DesignRush/Clutch/Sortlist/Semrush directory pages dominate these SERPs.
    - **Fix:** (a) get listed/optimized on every major directory (barnacle SEO — fast), (b) publish our own honest comparison + methodology content.

12. **Cost/ROI calculators surfaced as link magnets & featured-snippet bait.** R · Effort S — we *already have* the tools (an advantage most lack); we're under-linking/promoting them for backlinks and "calculator" queries.

### E. Off-site authority & local (**the real 1–3 month ranking bottleneck**)

13. **Directory citations + agency Google Business Profile.** R · Effort M
    - **Who has it:** essentially all ranked competitors sit on Clutch, UpCity, DesignRush, Sortlist, hellodarwin, Semrush, G2, plus a fully optimized GBP with reviews.
    - **Fix:** NAP-consistent citation blitz + claim/optimize GBP. Foundational local-ranking + referral traffic; fast to execute.

14. **Backlinks / digital PR.** R · Effort L (start now, compounds)
    - Established competitors out-rank us mostly on **domain authority/age + links.** Fastest ethical accelerators in 90 days: HARO/Connectively expert quotes, a data-study link magnet built from our own `/benchmarks` data, guest posts on Canadian marketing/industry blogs, local partnerships, podcast guesting. This is the single biggest lever for "rank faster than competitors."

### F. Conversion instrumentation

15. **Real phone + click-to-call + sticky mobile call bar.** C · Effort S 🔒 — competitors show phone prominently; `CONTENT-TODO` confirms we don't yet have a real number wired.
16. **Instant booking calendar (Calendly/Cal.com) on service pages.** C · Effort S — remove the form→wait→email friction.
17. **Live chat / chatbot** (speed-to-lead). C · Effort S.
18. **Analytics/pixels live (GA4, Meta Pixel, GSC, call tracking).** R+C · Effort S — per `CONTENT-TODO` these keys aren't in yet; **without GSC + GA4 we're flying blind on the very rankings we're trying to improve.** Non-negotiable week-1 item.

### G. Schema deltas (cheap, after the real data exists)
19. `Review` + `AggregateRating` (after real reviews) → SERP stars.
20. `LocalBusiness` w/ geo + hours + phone (agency + location pages).
21. `VideoObject` (after video). `HowTo`/`Article` on guides.

## 4. Prioritized 1–3 month roadmap (fastest impact first)

### 🔴 Weeks 1–2 — "Instrument + claim + anchor" (mostly S effort, huge leverage)
- Wire **GA4 + Google Search Console + Meta Pixel + call tracking** (item 18). *Everything else is measured against this.*
- Claim/optimize **Google Business Profile** + start a **review drive** with real clients (feeds items 1, 13, 19).
- Create/complete **Clutch, UpCity, DesignRush, Sortlist, hellodarwin, Semrush** profiles → **directory citations + award badges** (items 3, 13).
- Add **real phone + click-to-call + sticky mobile CTA + booking calendar + live chat** (items 15–17).
- Anchor the **free audit with a $ value** + add an honest **guarantee** line (items 7, 8).

### 🟠 Weeks 2–6 — "Money pages + proof"
- Publish **cost/pricing guides** per core service ("Google Ads cost Canada 2026," "Website design cost Toronto," "SEO pricing Canada," "Facebook ads cost," "social media management cost") (item 10) — **highest-ROI content sprint.**
- Publish **transparent pricing ranges / "starting at"** on `/pricing` + per service (item 9).
- Ship **2–3 named case studies** (client permission) + **real logo wall** + **1 testimonial video** (items 2, 4, 5).
- Turn on **Review/AggregateRating + LocalBusiness schema** once reviews are live (items 19–20).
- On-page pass: retitle for "cost/pricing/best/near me" intent; internal-link the new money pages.

### 🟡 Weeks 4–12 — "Authority (compounding)"
- **Digital PR / link building**: HARO/Connectively, a **/benchmarks-based data study** as a link magnet, Canadian guest posts, local partnerships, podcasts (item 14).
- **"Best [service] agency" comparison content** + keep expanding directory presence (item 11).
- Real **author bios + team photos** across service/blog for E-E-A-T (item 6).
- Content velocity: 2–4 posts/mo clustered around the money pages.

---

## 5. Honesty guardrails (per `CLAUDE.md`)
Items 1, 2, 4, 6, 15 require **real, permissioned data** (reviews, client names/logos, photos, phone). We **collect and display real proof** — we do **not** fabricate reviews, named clients, or turn on `AggregateRating` before it's verifiable. Guarantees (item 7) and pricing (item 9) must be **defensible** — ranges and honest terms, never "guaranteed #1."

## 6. One-line verdict
> We've already won the on-page/AEO game most Toronto competitors are still playing. To out-rank and out-convert them in **90 days**, stop adding page content and instead **(1) turn on measurement, (2) claim directories + gather real reviews, (3) ship bottom-funnel cost pages, (4) add named proof + conversion plumbing, and (5) start digital PR now.**
